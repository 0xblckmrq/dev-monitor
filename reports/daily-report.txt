  ## Links

  - **Bot overview:** https://github.com/0xblckmrq/human.tech.AI/blob/main/BOT_OVERVIEW.md

  ## What It Is

  A Discord AI support bot that answers community questions about human.tech products using a pre-built knowledge base. Powered by Claude Haiku (Anthropic API). Only answers from its knowledge base — redirects to a support ticket channel when it cannot answer.

  Supports both passive message-based responses and active /ask slash commands.

  ---

  ## Architecture (updated April 16, 2026)

  Scraping and serving are fully separated:

  ```
  scrape.js → kb.json → bot.js
  ```

  - **scrape.js** — standalone scraper: crawls websites, fetches Twitter/Nitter RSS, reads local docs, writes to kb.json. Incremental mode via content hashing. Puppeteer fallback for JS-rendered pages.
  - **bot.js** — loads kb.json on startup. Never scrapes. Answers from pre-built KB only.
  - **kb.json** — structured KB file per source: URL, type, content, word count, hash, timestamps.

  ---

  ## Current State
